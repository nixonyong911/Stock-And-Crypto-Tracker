## How AI Stock Analysis Works

Traditional market analysis requires hours of scanning charts, reading news, and interpreting indicators. Stock And Crypto Tracker automates this entire process and delivers the results in plain English — directly to your Telegram.

Here's how it works, step by step.

## Step 1: Data Collection

Every trading day, our system collects data for every ticker on your watchlist:

- OHLCV price data (Open, High, Low, Close, Volume)
- Candlestick pattern detection (engulfing, doji, hammer, etc.)
- Technical indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, moving averages)
- Market news filtered for relevance to your specific tickers
- Economic indicators that affect broader market sentiment

This data is collected from multiple sources and cross-validated for accuracy.

## Step 2: Pattern Recognition

Our AI engine analyzes the collected data through multiple lenses:

- Multi-timeframe alignment — are daily, weekly, and monthly charts telling the same story?
- Support and resistance levels computed from recent price action
- Volume analysis to confirm or reject price movements
- Candlestick patterns matched against historical outcomes
- News sentiment scored for impact on each specific ticker

## Step 3: Signal Generation

Based on the analysis, the system generates a signal for each ticker:

- Signal direction: Bullish, Bearish, or Neutral
- Confidence level: How strong is the evidence (Low to High)
- Risk assessment: Current risk level based on volatility and position
- Horizon: Expected timeframe for the signal (day trade, swing, long-term)
- Entry price, target price, and stop-loss levels

## Step 4: Plain-English Briefing

The signal and analysis are translated into a concise briefing with three key sections:

- What's happening — a summary of recent price action and why
- What to watch — upcoming catalysts, key levels, or events
- News factor — relevant headlines driving the move

## Step 5: Delivery via Telegram

Your briefing arrives in Telegram — no separate app, no dashboard to check. Open the message, read for 30 seconds, and you're informed.

## Why This Approach Works

Most retail investors don't have time to do institutional-level analysis. But they still need to make informed decisions about their portfolio. By automating the analysis pipeline and translating it into plain language, we bridge the gap between professional-grade insights and accessibility.
