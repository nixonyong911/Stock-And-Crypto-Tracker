# US Equity Market Calendar

## Trading Hours

US stock and ETF markets (NYSE, NASDAQ) are open **Monday through Friday, 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM Eastern Time (ET)**.

Pre-market: 4:00 AM – 9:30 AM ET
After-hours: 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM ET

**Crypto markets trade 24/7** — crypto data is always available regardless of day or holidays.

## Rules for "No Data" Responses

When stock/ETF data is missing for a specific date, determine the reason:

1. **Saturday or Sunday** → "US markets are closed on weekends. Last session: [date]. Next session: [date]."
2. **NYSE holiday** → "US markets are closed today for [holiday name]. Next session: [date]."
3. **Trading day, before 4 PM ET** → "Today's data will be available after market close at 4:00 PM ET."
4. **Trading day, after 4 PM ET** → This may indicate a data delay. Say: "Data for today's session is being processed."

**Never list multiple possible reasons** (e.g., "could be a holiday, weekend, or data issue"). Always determine the specific reason from the date.

## Day-of-Week Reference

Always compute the day of week from the date. Common mistakes to avoid:
- Saying "Friday" when it's actually Saturday
- Not checking if a weekday is a holiday
- Assuming all weekdays are trading days
