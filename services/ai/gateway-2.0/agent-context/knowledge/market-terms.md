# Financial Markets Knowledge Reference

## Candlestick Patterns

### Doji Variants
| Pattern | Description | Signal |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Doji** | Open ≈ Close, small body | Indecision, potential reversal |
| **Long-legged Doji** | Long upper and lower wicks | Strong indecision |
| **Dragonfly Doji** | Long lower wick, no upper wick | Bullish reversal (at support) |
| **Gravestone Doji** | Long upper wick, no lower wick | Bearish reversal (at resistance) |

### Reversal Patterns
| Pattern | Description | Signal |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Hammer** | Small body at top, long lower wick (2x+ body) | Bullish reversal after downtrend |
| **Inverted Hammer** | Small body at bottom, long upper wick | Potential bullish reversal |
| **Shooting Star** | Small body at bottom, long upper wick | Bearish reversal after uptrend |
| **Engulfing Bullish** | Green candle fully engulfs prior red | Strong bullish reversal |
| **Engulfing Bearish** | Red candle fully engulfs prior green | Strong bearish reversal |

### Momentum Patterns
| Pattern | Description | Signal |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Marubozu Bullish** | Full green body, no wicks | Strong buying pressure |
| **Marubozu Bearish** | Full red body, no wicks | Strong selling pressure |
| **Spinning Top** | Small body, equal upper/lower wicks | Indecision, weakening trend |

## Pattern Signals Explained

### Bullish Reversal
- Occurs after a downtrend
- Indicates buyers stepping in
- Look for: hammer, dragonfly doji, engulfing bullish
- Confirm with: increased volume, support level

### Bearish Reversal
- Occurs after an uptrend
- Indicates sellers taking control
- Look for: shooting star, gravestone doji, engulfing bearish
- Confirm with: increased volume, resistance level

### Continuation Patterns
- Form during a pause in the trend
- Trend likely to resume in same direction
- Smaller body candles within the trend

### Indecision Signals
- Doji and spinning tops
- Neither buyers nor sellers in control
- Often precedes a reversal or breakout

## Market Terminology

### Price Action
- **Support**: Price level where buying pressure prevents further decline
- **Resistance**: Price level where selling pressure prevents further rise
- **Breakout**: Price moving above resistance or below support
- **Pullback**: Temporary reversal against the main trend

### Candle Anatomy
- **Body**: Rectangle between open and close prices
- **Body Size**: |Close - Open|, larger = stronger conviction
- **Upper Wick**: High - max(Open, Close)
- **Lower Wick**: min(Open, Close) - Low
- **Range**: High - Low (total candle height)

### Bullish vs Bearish
- **Bullish**: Close > Open (green/white candle), expecting price rise
- **Bearish**: Close < Open (red/black candle), expecting price fall
- **Bullish Ratio**: % of stocks with bullish candles on a given day

### Volume Analysis
- **Volume**: Number of shares traded
- **Above average volume**: Confirms pattern validity
- **Volume spike**: Sudden increase, often at reversals
- **Declining volume**: Weakening trend, potential reversal

## Interpreting the Data

### Strong Signals (High Confidence)
- Large body size (top 20% for the day)
- Volume > 1.5x average
- Pattern at key support/resistance
- Multiple patterns detected

### Weak Signals (Low Confidence)
- Small body size
- Below average volume
- No clear support/resistance context
- Isolated pattern without confirmation

### Using Bullish/Bearish Ratio
| Ratio | Market Condition |
|-------|------------------|
| > 60% bullish | Strong uptrend, look for pullback entries |
| 50-60% bullish | Mild bullish, trend continuation likely |
| 40-50% bullish | Neutral/consolidating |
| < 40% bullish | Bearish sentiment, caution on longs |
